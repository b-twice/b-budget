About

It's easy to spend money but it's hard to keep track of where it all goes. While there are other money management services out there, I wanted complete control of my finances and to answer atypical questions such as "Which grocery store has the cheapest olive oil?" Below is a tour of what I've put together:

Expenses

Any budget must start with answering the question, "Is my spending in the budget?" See how much you are spending by category, filter by month, and figure out if you are over or under budget.

Groceries

View your grocery purchases and compare them by store.
Is organic broccolli worth twice as much? Some questions don't have an easy answer.

Data Management

Use the grocery cart to upload weekly grocery purchases and categorize where they were bought them and whether they are organic or seasonal.

Data Views

Search and filter your transactions or look at graphs to compare your spending over the years.
