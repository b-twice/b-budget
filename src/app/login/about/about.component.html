What is this?

This is my website to shine a light on the void that my money goes into. It is surprisingly tricky to piece this all together and to figure things out like which grocery store has the cheapest canned tomatoes. Although you cannot log-in, since I'm the only user, here is a tour of the features:

Any budget must start with answering the question, "is my spending in the budget?" See how much you are spending by category, filter by month, and figure out if you are over or under budget.
Get some perspective on your groceries by product and grocery store.
Is organic broccolli worth twice as much? Some questions don't have an easy answer.
Do CRUD things like the joy of uploading your weekly groceries.
Search and filter your transactions or look at graphs to compare your spending over the years.
Lastly, look wistfully at that time you read the Wheel of Time and do things that have nothing to do with budgeting, such as what you've been reading.
