What is this?

Spending is easy. Tracking is difficult. This is my attempt to figure out all sorts of things like following a budget, figuring out which groceries are the cheapest, and seeing what's being spent where. Below is a tour of what all goes on inside:

Expenses

Any budget must start with answering the question, "is my spending in the budget?" See how much you are spending by category, filter by month, and figure out if you are over or under budget.

Groceries

View your grocery purchases and compare them by store.
Is organic broccolli worth twice as much? Some questions don't have an easy answer.

Data Management

Use the grocery cart to upload your weekly grocery purchases and categorize where you bought them and whether they are organic or seasonal.

Data Views

Search and filter your transactions or look at graphs to compare your spending over the years.

And more...
